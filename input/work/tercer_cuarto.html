Tercer Cuarto

I think this is the biggest project y made as freelance, my first (and unique) hybrid app, last year I meet Angularjs and I ended falling in love with the new form of making web apps, and with Ionic well, the perfect combo, so I decided to make Tercer Cuarto as a Hybrid App

Tercer Cuarto is FREE :)


Tercer Cuarto Admin

To update the info, scores and galleries, i made a simple CMS with CodeIgniter that changes the JSON who gives data to the App, is not real-time but if you refresh the view can see changes immediately, also i created a cron who make another JSON with the last 5 tweets of @tercer_cuarto every 2 minutes.

Also I remember the app got into the top 100 of free sports apps category in Play Store, i don't have a good screenshot just this photo that i friend sent me LOL.
